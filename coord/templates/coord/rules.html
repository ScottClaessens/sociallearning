Market Rules

In this experiment, two Crops are available: Melons and Pumpkins.

In principle, one of the two Crops (Melons or Pumpkins) has a higher value than the other Crop.

Yet, the Supplier of the Crop seeds will give more discount if more Farmers decide to produce the same Crop.

This means that the revenues of your Crops also depend on the decisions of the other Farmers in your group: the more Farmers decide to produce a specific Crop, the higher its revenues.
