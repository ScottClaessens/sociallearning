Market Rules

In this experiment, Apples are the only Crop available.

Each Season, you can decide if you want to spray Pesticides during the production of your Apples.

Using Pesticides will increase your land production.

However, the more Farmers decide to use Pesticides, the lower the value of all Apples on the Market (both Sprayed Apples and Unsprayed Apples).
